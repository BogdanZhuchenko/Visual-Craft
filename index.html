About Us

We believe that small can be big

Sparkinity was born because we believe in innovation.

Data shows that small stores are better positioned than ever to take on retail

giants as consumers increasingly seek curation and a personalised touch. Agility is a powerful weapon and we seek to empower the agile.

But to fulfil our vision we need to disrupt the age old system of distribution &

wholesale. Our founding team has personal experiences & understanding of the expenses incurred , time and incredible effort that both buyers & makers make to connect. All because of the need to get a physical glimpse of that unicorn called the bestseller before risking an order.

​We are changing the buying process by providing retailers a medium to order online with free returns. Makers save on huge upfront costs for creating

distribution networks & store owners can easily let their customers decide without taking on the risks involved with buying decisions.
